Biography   (CV)


I am a first year Ph.D student at School of Informatics and Computing, Indiana University Bloomington. My advisor is Dr. Xiaozhong Liu. I am interested in information retrieval, data mining, machine learning and natural language processing. I am constantly working on modifying algorithm to retrieve better feedback.


I hold a master degree at School of Information Science, University of Pittsburgh. My advisor was Dr. Vladimir Zadorozhny and Dr. Daqing He. I once followed Dr. Daqing He to go on collaborative research and Dr. Rosta Farzan to create a website for volunteer users.


I received my BS degree of Shanghai International Studies University in 2009. And my major was Information Management & Information System. I got a lot of internship experience in different areas, such as Bank of China, E&Y and Messe Frankfurt GmbH.


Till now, I have several web application projects which can be used in expert finding, used car sales and seeking job area. My specialize is information retrieval. I will attend 2014 TREC Conference in November 19 - 21, held by NIST. And my paper about ad hoc twitter retrieval will be published after the meeting. Hope I can share ideas with all you guys at that time!

Research Interest:


  • Collaborative search result modified based on google result through users chat log, query history, clickthrough history and snippet.
  • Ad hoc microblog information retrieval by using modified retrieval model and query & document expansion.
  • Interactive website design and creation.
